Welcome to Prepare For Exams!
We're a team of passionate educators and tech enthusiasts who believe that practicing with past papers and questions is the key to acing your exams. Our mission is to make exam preparation easy, effective, and accessible for students all around the world.

With our comprehensive question bank, categorized by topic and difficulty level, you can practice with thousands of questions from different exam boards and subjects. We've also included markschemes for each question, so you can instantly check your answers and monitor your progress.

But that's not all - we've also added a section for revision notes! Here, you'll find concise and easy-to-understand summaries of key concepts and topics from different subjects. Our revision notes are perfect for last-minute reviews or quick refreshers.

Prepare for Exams also allows you to take notes online for each question, so you can keep track of your thought process, key concepts, and strategies. Your notes will be autosaved, so you can always come back to them later.

We're committed to providing you with the most up-to-date and relevant materials. That's why we regularly update our question bank and revision notes to ensure that our resources are aligned with the latest exam trends and requirements.

At Prepare for Exams, we're dedicated to helping you achieve your academic goals.
